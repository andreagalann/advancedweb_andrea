My name is Andrea. I’m a graphic designer, independent and multidisciplinary by nature, specialized in editorial design, art direction and digital content. From the beginning, design was never just about visuals for me, but about meaning — understanding context, shaping concepts and building identities that communicate with intention.

I work closely with clients, valuing collaboration as an essential part of the process. I believe design should never exist without purpose. Every project needs direction, structure and a strong visual language to speak clearly and stand out authentically.
